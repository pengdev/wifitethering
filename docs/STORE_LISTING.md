## Short Description
The simplest one-tap WiFi Hotspot toggle. Now with smart scheduling and device tracking.

## Full Description
**Take control of your mobile hotspot with Tethering Shortcut.**

Tired of digging through layers of settings just to share your internet? Tethering Shortcut provides a beautiful, modern interface to manage your WiFi hotspot with a single tap.

Whether you're sharing data with your tablet, laptop, or a friend, our app makes it effortless, efficient, and smart.

### 🌟 Key Features
- **One-Tap Toggle:** Create your own WiFi spot instantly with a single button.
- **Smart Scheduler:** Plan your hotspot usage. Set reminders to enable or disable your hotspot at specific times (Perfect for commuters and work-from-home!).
- **Connected Devices List:** See exactly who is using your data. Track connected devices with IP and MAC address details.
- **Session Tracking:** Monitor how long your hotspot has been active to manage battery and data usage.
- **Modern Design:** A clean, Jetpack Compose-based Material 3 interface with Dark Mode and Indigo aesthetics.

### 💎 Premium Features (One-Time Purchase)
- **Zero Ads:** Remove all banners and interruptions.
- **Advanced Scheduler:** Unlimited scheduling reminders.
- **AMOLED Dark Mode:** Save battery with a true black theme.
- **Custom Config:** Quick access to edit your Hotspot Name (SSID) and Password (available on supported devices).

### 🛠 Why use Tethering Shortcut?
Most phones hide tethering settings deep in the system menu. This app was built to solve that "simple" problem reliably. It's lightweight, respects your privacy, and doesn't require any unnecessary permissions.

*Note: For Android 8.0 (Oreo) and above, the app provides a direct shortcut to the system tethering settings as required by Google Play security policies.*
